D. Signer has a background in both design and engineering. They received her BS in Industrial Design from the Georgia Institute of Technology and her MS in Interaction Design from Carnegie Mellon University. They has worked as a designer at a variety of companies, including Armitage, 3Jane, and Tessier-Ashpool. In addition to their professional work, D. Signer is also an active member of the online design community, sharing their work and ideas with others who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of interaction design.
